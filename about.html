About Us:

Haram sweets & Bakers founded in 1976. We have been serving our valuable custumers since last 44 years. Our slogan is "Quality over quantity always".
Our mission is to provide best quality products for very reasonable prices to fulfil our custumers' life with sweetness, and ensure that the every member of our society can enjoy the beautiful moments of life. Most importantly we ensure to maintain the best quality, freshness and taste of our products. We had started our business from a small shop but now by tha grace of Allah, we own two branches in heart of the city. We use natural and organic ingredients to process our all products.
